Drag the mouse 	- 	Adjust the perspective yourself
KEY_A		-	Start/Close the animation
KEY_R		-	Reset the perspective
KEY_G		-	Turn on/off Earth's gravity simulator
KEY_T		-	Turn on/off the Space Train
KEY_UP		-	Speed up the Space Train
KEY_DOWN	-	Speed down the Space Train
KEY_NUMPAD8	-	Control the astronaut to move up
KEY_NUMPAD2	-	Control the astronaut to move down
KEY_NUMPAD4	-	Control the astronaut to move left
KEY_NUMPAD6	-	Control the astronaut to move right
KEY_W		-	Control the astronaut to move forward
KEY_S		-	Control the astronaut to move backward
